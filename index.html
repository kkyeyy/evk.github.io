    Things you can do here!

  • Long Kang Fishing

    • If you grew up catching fishes in the neighbourhood's canals and streams, Qian Hu's longkang fishing area will let you relive the joy and excitement of this popular childhood activity.

    Kids will have a lot of fun trying to catch the little fishes in this area which features pools of water that are made to look like a natural stream.

  • Retail Shopping

    • Get everything you need to keep pet fishes at home right here!

    There are fish tanks, filtration, pumps, decorations and feed available. Definitely not forgetting the main character - Ornamental fishes!

    You can also purchase fish feed to feed the koi and turtles in our pond!
